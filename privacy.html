Privacy Policy

Greetings from SHEQ Consulting (hereafter referred to as "The Firm," "we," "us," or "our"). We are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled with utmost care and responsibility. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, utilization, disclosure, and protection of your personal information when you browse our website, utilize our services, or engage with us in any capacity.

Data We Gather

We accumulate various categories of information to deliver and enhance our services. This encompasses:

  • Individual Details: This includes your full name, residential address, contact number, email address, birth date, national identification number, and other identifiers that can directly or indirectly distinguish you.
  • Monetary Information: This encompasses bank account details, credit card information, financial records, tax identification numbers, and other fiscal data necessary for providing our consultancy services.
  • Professional Background: This includes your career history, educational qualifications, and other information pertinent to your professional expertise.

How We Utilize Your Data

We employ the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To offer consultancy services, process transactions, and administer your account.
  • Correspondence: To keep you informed about our services, updates, and promotional offerings.
  • Enhancement: To refine our website, services, and overall client experience.
  • Regulatory Adherence: To meet legal obligations, prevent fraudulent activities, and ensure the security of our services.

We may share your information with:

  • Third-party service providers who assist us in delivering services, such as IT support, payment processing, and marketing initiatives.
  • Business partners with whom we collaborate to offer services or products.
  • When mandated by law or to safeguard our rights and ensure safety.

Safeguarding Your Information

We implement various security measures to ensure the protection of your personal data:

  • Data Encryption: All sensitive information transmitted online is encrypted using state-of-the-art secure socket layer (SSL) technology.
  • Access Restrictions: Only authorized personnel have access to your personal information, and they are bound by strict confidentiality obligations.
  • Periodic Assessments: We conduct regular evaluations of our security protocols to ensure the ongoing protection of your data.

Information Sharing Practices

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to external parties except under the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business and delivering services to you.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information when required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.
  • Corporate Transactions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

Your Data Rights

You possess the following rights concerning your personal information:

  • Access: The right to review your personal information.
  • Rectification: The right to amend inaccuracies in your personal information.
  • Erasure: The right to request the removal of your personal information.
  • Opposition: The right to object to the processing of your personal information.
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your personal information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

Digital Tracking Technologies

Our website employs cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You have the option to manage your preferences for these technologies through your browser settings.

Amendments to this Statement

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any modifications will be published on this page, and we will notify you of significant changes via email or through our website.

Get in Touch

If you have any inquiries or feedback regarding our Privacy Policy, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at info@sheqconsult.co.za.
